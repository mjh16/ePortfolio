Example Assignment: Viral Marketing Campaign

In this project you will develop and propose a DIY viral marketing campaign for your Project 1 website. This marketing campaign should be aimed at reaching the target audience for the site, and expanding the reach through viral circulation and remediation of materials from project 1.  This project will be accomplished in four parts.

Part 1 - Planning

Your team will develop a marketing plan and submit a proposal for that plan. It will include marketing via social networks, digital tools, and material artifacts. In this plan you will propose how your marketing strategy will engage it at least 3 different spaces (of which one is material and one is digital) and 3 different interfaces. You will be asked to articulate how you plan to have your marketing materials circulate, and how you will account for their circulation and engagement with the audience(s).

This proposal should be at least 250 words, provide some examples, and be specific to the ways in which you are engaging with the different spaces. This proposal should include ways in which you might track the engagement with the marketing materials and the impact of  your campaign.

Part 2 - Pitch

You will compose, make, and pitch your viral marketing campaign to the audience of our class. In this pitch you will use drafts of marketing materials and audience analysis to identify and reach a targeted audience for your website. You will propose ways in which this marketing may circulate within and beyond your targeting audience, and plan for ways to track that circulation and engagement. Key questions to think about: how might you market your website? Who comprises the audience(s) for this site? How might you drive engagement? How might you track this engagement?

Part 3 - Debrief

Your team will collaboratively author a report on your proposed viral marketing campaign, accounting for interactions, influence, and engagement with your audience(s). This report will use data and information gathered from your pitch, and proposing ways in which you may revise this campaign and produce materials prior to implementation. This debrief report will also take into consideration theoretical materials and readings from the unit. Your collaborative task involves also framing the your pitch and new plans within the context of this class.

Part 4 - Individual Reflection

In this individual reflection you will consider your role within the team, and reflect on the composing and editing that you did within this project. Furthermore, you will also reconsider your theory of composing, thinking about how the action with networks and circulation influenced your experiences with composing.

Timeline

March 6th-April 12th: Create marketing materials, circulate to other groups, solicit feedback, and revise based on the feedback

Sunday April 19th: Submit Debrief Report Draft and final Artifacts

Sunday April 26th: Submit Final Debrief and Individual Reflection
