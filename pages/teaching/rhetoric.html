Example Assignment: Collaborative Rhetorical Analysis of a Contemporary Issue

Your Task

In this collaborative—which may be complete in a group of up to three—you will analyze a current event or issue using a modern or post-modern rhetorical theory, concept, framework, or theorist of your choosing. You will then present your analysis through a genre that is relevant to the contemporary event or technology.

Objectives

  • To research and use a rhetorical theory to analyze a contemporary issue or technology.
  • Come to a better understanding of how to use rhetorical theory as a means of analyzing and producing analysis.
  • Better understand how rhetorical analysis can help us develop knowledge about the world.
  • Present the knowledge gained from analysis in a way that can be engaging for a targeted audience.

Requirements

  1. Focuses primarily on a rhetorical analysis using a frame of your choosing.
  2. Clearly and coherently presents the results of analysis in a relevant genre.
  3. The analysis reveals some new knowledge about the chosen issue or event.

Assessment

  • Articulation of the knowledge developed through the analysis
  • Clarity and Cohesion of the presentation
  • Completeness of the project - does it meet all the assignment requirements
