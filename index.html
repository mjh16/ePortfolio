Michael Healy's ePortfolio

PhD Candidate Florida State University
Rhetoric and Composition

Teacher, Scholar and Writer interested in the intersections of technology, composition, rhetoric, and undergraduate education. Working on a dissertation project titled Tracing Techne that utilizes Digital Humanities methodologies to curate and trace intersections of techne, text-technologies, and creativity in published scholarship from the 1990s. Currently teaching Introduction to the History of Text Technologies. Michael is currently a graduate co-administrator for the Museum of Everyday Writing.
